DebtFree

CTOS Smart Debt Settlement Planner

About This Project

DebtFree is a client-side financial planning tool that parses your CTOS/CCRIS credit report data and generates a personalized debt settlement roadmap. It handles Written-Off (W) accounts, Full & Final Settlement (F&F) offers, Trade Defaults, and Active Loans with strategy-based payoff projections.

Features

  • 4-step wizard: Income → Commitments → Debts → Plan
  • CTOS-specific debt statuses: Active, Written-Off, F&F, Trade Default
  • Private CTOS JSON upload from your local device
  • Avalanche vs Snowball strategy comparison
  • Month-by-month payment schedule with interest tracking
  • Interactive charts: payoff timeline & payment breakdown
  • Financial health check with debt-to-income ratio
  • CTOS score recovery guidance and AKPK referral
  • F&F negotiation range calculator (30-70%)
  • Print-ready report output
  • 100% client-side — no data leaves your browser

Tech Stack

How to Use

  1. Open index.html in any modern browser
  2. Enter your income details and monthly commitments
  3. Upload your CTOS data JSON from your local device or add debts manually
  4. Click "Generate Plan" to get your personalized roadmap
  5. Compare strategies, review the schedule, and follow the action plan

Privacy

All calculations run entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server, API, or third party. Your financial information never leaves your device.
